FAQ

What's your current tech stack?
I mostly work with Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, the basics are still my favorite. You can build almost anything with them if you know them well. On top of that, I often use React/Next.js, Angular, TypeScript, and TailwindCSS because they make modern web projects faster and cleaner to build. I’m not tied to any single stack. I also like to self-host my stuff, whether it’s on AWS, Vercel, or my own server.
What kind of projects do you like working on?
Anything that feels real and has users who care. I like simple, useful tools more than fancy over-engineered stuff. If it involves the web, I’m usually interested.
Do you use AI tools or code assistants?
Yeah, sometimes. They’re great for speeding things up, but I still prefer to understand what’s happening under the hood. I trust my brain more than any model.
What’s your opinion on frameworks vs vanilla?
Frameworks are great for moving fast, but vanilla JS, HTML, and CSS are the real roots. If you understand those well, you can pick up any framework easily, or even build your own.
